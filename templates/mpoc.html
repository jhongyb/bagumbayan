Mission

We SHAPE-UP Bagumbayan by building a framework that embodies sustainability through mechanism of:

1. Institutionalize by providing education and training to farmer-tiller;

2. Deliver health care services rigth to the doorstep of the residents of far-flung areas through the establishment of Integrated Health Care Programs;

3. Provide an enabling environment for professional growth, one that targets the bridging of compentency gaps in every calling within the municipality through academic and technical educationand skills development;

4. Recognize our identities as a tri-people and institutionalize commonalities by establishing an avenue for dialogues prior to implementation;

5. Establish tree farming in agricultural development, enhance biodiversity in the protected forest and production forest land;

6. Adapt convergent approaches in entrepreneurship, business and industry through providing windows for networking and promotions;

7. Promote transparent, accountable and participative governance through strict compliance to existing laws, rules and regulations;and

8. Maintain peace and order,and ensure public safety through mainstreaming human security and conflict sensitivity local governance with active support to citizenry.


Vision

By 2028, the Municipality of Bagumbayanis an Agri-ecotourism Hub of Central Mindanao, home of healthy, empowered and peace loving citizenry living in a resilient, ecologically balanced, gender-sensitive and progressive community governed by morally upright leadership.
